# Hypothesis: Curator Role

AI-assisted development may benefit from a Curator role distinct from planner, executor, and reviewer.

The Curator maintains continuity by observing changes, identifying stale knowledge, and proposing evidence-backed relationships.

The Curator should remain advisory, not authoritative.

## Possible Responsibilities

- Detect stale documentation.
- Connect ERPs to commits.
- Connect ADRs to implementation.
- Identify architectural drift.
- Suggest missing graph relationships.
- Surface repeated review findings.
- Recommend when practice should become principle.
