# Hypothesis: Engineering Knowledge Graph

Token Shuffle may evolve beyond token savings into a system that helps preserve engineering continuity across human and AI contributors.

The hypothesis is that project knowledge is not only contained in documents, but in the relationships between them.

If validated, Token Shuffle may help future contributors understand not only what the project currently believes, but how those beliefs came to exist.

## Central Claim

Engineering knowledge is not contained in documents alone.

It emerges from the relationships between evidence, decisions, observations, and their evolution over time.
