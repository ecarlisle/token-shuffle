# Hypothesis: Engineering Precedent

Software projects develop precedent over time.

Precedent is the accumulated engineering reasoning that emerges from decisions, reviews, implementations, failures, validations, and supersessions.

Unlike an ADR, precedent is not authored directly. It emerges from relationships across historical evidence.

## Possible Precedent States

- Strong precedent
- Weak precedent
- Conflicting precedent
- Superseded precedent
- Reinforced precedent
- Decaying precedent
