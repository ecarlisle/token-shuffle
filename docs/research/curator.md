# Curator Role

## Hypothesis

AI-assisted development may need a Curator role distinct from planner, executor, and reviewer.

The Curator maintains continuity.

## Responsibilities

A Curator may help:

- detect stale documentation
- connect ERPs to commits
- connect ADRs to implementation
- identify architectural drift
- suggest missing relationships
- surface repeated review findings
- recommend when practice should become principle

## Boundaries

The Curator should be advisory, not authoritative.

It should observe, infer carefully, and ask before codifying new engineering doctrine.
