# Engineering Jurisprudence

Engineering jurisprudence is the study of how engineering precedent forms, evolves, conflicts, and becomes superseded.

The analogy is common-law precedent.

Earlier decisions do not make future decisions automatic, but they influence reasoning.

## Key Ideas

- Precedent
- Supersession
- Distinguishing prior decisions
- Conflicting precedent
- Reinforcing evidence
- Decaying precedent

## Research Question

Can software projects preserve precedent in a useful, inspectable way?
