# Experiment 0001: Graph Relationships

## Question

Can Token Shuffle represent relationships between ERPs, ADRs, Engineering Reviews, commits, releases, and validation evidence?

## Success Criteria

- Relationships can be represented in a simple, inspectable format.
- At least one ERP can be linked to implementation and validation evidence.
- Human review can approve or reject inferred relationships.

## Failure Criteria

- Relationships require excessive manual maintenance.
- The graph becomes noisy or difficult to trust.
