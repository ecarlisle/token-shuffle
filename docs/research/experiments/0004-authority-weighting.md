# Experiment 0004: Authority Weighting

## Question

Can Token Shuffle rank context by authority without ignoring fresh evidence?

## Success Criteria

- High-authority artifacts are prioritized when relevant.
- Fresh observations still surface when they directly affect the task.
- Context selection is explainable.

## Failure Criteria

- Old decisions dominate despite contradictory current evidence.
- Fresh but low-authority signals cause excessive churn.
