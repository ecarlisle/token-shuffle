# Experiment 0003: Precedent Inference

## Question

Can repeated engineering decisions and review findings reveal useful project precedent?

## Success Criteria

- Repeated patterns can be identified across reviews, ADRs, ERPs, and releases.
- Suggested precedent remains explainable.
- Humans can approve, reject, or refine inferred precedent.

## Failure Criteria

- Inferred precedent becomes speculative.
- The system overstates weak evidence.
