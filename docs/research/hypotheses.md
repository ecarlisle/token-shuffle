# Research Hypotheses

## H1: Token savings is a foundation, not the full product

Token Shuffle may begin as a token savings proxy while later supporting broader engineering continuity.

## H2: Documentation alone is insufficient

Documents preserve knowledge, but the application can add value by maintaining relationships between documents, code, tests, commits, and releases.

## H3: The graph is more valuable than retrieval alone

Retrieving relevant files is useful. Understanding how those files relate may be more valuable.

## H4: The Curator role may reduce architectural drift

A Curator that observes changes and proposes evidence-backed relationships may help preserve engineering consistency across different AI contributors.

## H5: Research must not outrun evidence

These ideas should remain research until validated by implementation, review, and practical use.
